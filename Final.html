Vegan blueberry pancakes

Vegan blueberry pancakes are fluffy, moist, and bursting with sweet-tart blueberry flavor. They're a perfect indulgence for a leisurely weekend brunch or any morning when you're craving something special. Plus, they're easy to customize with your favorite toppings or mix-ins, making them a versatile and satisfying breakfast option for vegans and non-vegans alike. Enjoy!

Pressure cooker refried beans

Pressure cooker refried beans are creamy, savory, and packed with flavor from the onions, garlic, and spices. The pressure cooking process helps to tenderize the beans quickly, resulting in a dish that's just as delicious as the traditional version but much faster to prepare. Plus, making them at home allows you to customize the seasoning and adjust the texture to your preference. Enjoy!

José Pizarro’s carajamandanga (chilled tomato soup)

Chilled tomato soup is a wonderful way to showcase the vibrant flavors of ripe summer tomatoes and fresh vegetables. It's light, healthy, and bursting with the essence of summer, making it a perfect dish for picnics, backyard barbecues, or any occasion when you want to beat the heat with a refreshing and satisfying meal.
